Frequently Asked Question

Clearing the Temporary Internet Files is just freeing your computer of unwanted temporary files that have been saved to your computer as you surf from one page to another. You will not be deleting any important files, so long as you did not deliberately save them there. The benefit of clearing the Temporary Internet Files is to free up additional space. On top of that, everytime you visit POEMS or other webpages, your computer will display the most current data available.

  • Open Internet Explorer
  • Click on "Tools".
  • Click on "Internet Options".
  • Click on "General" tab.
  • Click on "Delete Cookies" button.
  • Click "OK" in the prompt message to begin deleting the cookies.
  • Click on "Delete Files..." button.
  • In the prompt message place tick on "Delete all offline content", then click OK.
  • Click on "Apply" button to save the changes.

Automatically empty Temporary Internet Files folder everytime you closed the Internet Explorer browser.

  • Open Internet Explorer
  • Click on "Tools".
  • Click on "Internet Options".
  • Click on "Advance" tab.
  • Scroll all the way down the list.
  • Under "Security", place a tick in "Empty Temporary Internet Files Folder when browser is closed".
  • Click on "Aplly" button.

To access liveprices or charts, you will have to ensure that all forms of Java is enabled and the CSK slingshot program is downloaded from our site. Whenever you access liveprices, the CSK pop-up box will automatically be displayed for download.

If it doesn't automatically download /display, you will have to check the following:

STEP 1: Check and Enable Java Plus Remove CSK Entries.

  • Open Internet Explorer
  • Click on "Tools".
  • Click on "Internet Options".
  • Click on "Security" tab.
  • Click once on "Internet" icon.
  • Click on "Default Level" button
  • Click on "Advance" tab
  • Scroll down to find "Microsoft VM."
  • Scroll down and look for "Microsoft VM".
  • As shown above, place 3 ticks in the boxes: "Java Console", "Java Logging", "JIT Compiler", if any remove the tick in "Java (Sun)".
  • Click on "Apply" button to save the changes.
  • Click on "Content" tab
  • Click on "Publisher" button
  • Click on "Trusted publishers" tab.
  • Check if there is any "CSK" entries listed in the box.
  • Click on "Remove" button.
  • Repeat Steps 15-16 to remove any "CSK" entries left.
  • Click on "Tools".
  • Click Close to close window and "Internet Options" to complete.

STEP 2: Clear Temporary Internet Files

  • Open Internet Explorer
  • Click on "Tools".
  • Click on "Internet Options".
  • Click on "General" tab.
  • Click on "Delete Cookies" button.
  • Click "OK" in the prompt message to begin deleting the cookies.
  • Click on "Delete Files..." button.
  • In the prompt message place tick on "Delete all offline content", then click OK.
  • Click on "Apply" button to save the changes.

The cause of Blank Screen (Viewer Not Found) when loading the Live Prices could be due to lack of a Java software in your computer or outdated Java software.

After clicking on live prices, the program seems to be downloading the quotes but after some time it comes up with a message "Connection Time-out Reconnecting".

Solution :

  • Press the "Stop" button found on your browser.
  • Click on "Live Price" again to let the browser reload the page.
  • Do not click on anything else meanwhile and wait 4-5 mins.

If the live prices still do not load up within 4 - 5 mins, you will have to check on the ISP (internet service provider - Indonet, Biznet, CBN etc) that you are using and contact our technical officers at 57900900 or email to feedback@phillip.co.id for further assistance.

Take note that for users behind the Company Firewall, ie. accessing POEMS liveprices through their company connection, live prices are usually disabled by the individual company firewall. You may wish to contact your company's system administrator regarding this.
