POEMS ProFunds: Reap Your Investments from Handpicked Funds


POEMS ProFunds is an online mutual fund store provided by Phillip Sekuritas Indonesia. Integrated with POEMS, our online share trading platform, POEMS ProFunds provides the convenience of one-stop shopping and easy online access. Our partnerships with prominent asset management firms open up the opportunity to choose from a select group of mutual funds.


Why Use POEMS ProFunds?


  • Integrated with POEMS – POEMS ProFunds is integrated with POEMS, our online share trading platform, giving you access to mutual fund subscriptions as well as to stock trading.
  • Optimize the Use of Your Account – With choices of quality mutual funds at your disposal, you can optimize the use of your trading account by subscribing to mutual funds with idle capital in your trading account.
  • Segregated Client Accounts – In creating safe and secure investment environment, our client funds are held in segregated investor accounts (“Rekening Dana Investor”, or “RDI”) with top tiered banks.
  • Low Subscription Fee – From as low as 0%*
  • Track Mutual Funds Performance with One Platform – Track and compare the performance of our handpicked mutual funds through one platform. Read prospectus of each mutual fund from a single platform.
  • Save Time and Costs – Subscribe to mutual funds and redeem your mutual funds online to save time and costs.
